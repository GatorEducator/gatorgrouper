Developer Story

Professors and students alike at Allegheny College have previously struggled with sucessfully creating functional student groups, while maintaining productivity.

Group organization is often a challenging experience that frequently results in conflicts among students, unproductive group members, as well as loss of time.

The Software Engineering team at Allegheny College eventually determined that current grouping methods are ineffective and sought to develop a program that would allow for productive, functional and effortless group organization.

Product Desrcription

Gator Grouper is a web application that assists educators by organzing students into groups. Group organization is determined by the students' skill levels and user input.

To use this tool, one must sign in to either a GitHub or Google account by using the designated buttons above. After login is completed, educators have access to creating classes, assignments, as well as utilizing group assembly and organization.

Gator Grouper is designed to create fair and balanced groups effortlessly. Individuals that are interested are encouraged to take advantage of this powerful and user-friendly tool.
